Real-Time Forecasts in Yolo County

This platform provides a forecast of Yolo County hydrological conditions and water supply operations for the current water year. The forecasts are constructed using NOAA climate models, reservoir operations data, and groundwater management strategies.

Photo: Capay Dam. Credit: Yolo County Flood Control and Water Conservation District.

📊

Runoff Predictions

Constructed using meteorological information derived from the NOAA's National Multi-Model Ensemble (NMME) as processed by UC Merced's Climatology Lab

💧

Operations Modeling

Powered by the Yolo SGA model for the Yolo Subbasin Groundwater Agency Sustainable Groundwater Management Act process

🔍

Multi-Source Data

Integrates reservoir levels, groundwater storage, and precipitation patterns
